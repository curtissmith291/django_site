Select the key using the dropdown menu.
Input the desired fret range; enter the lower fret (smaller number) in the "Lower Fret" field, enter the higher fret in the "Higher Fret" field.
Diatonic chords for the selected key that fall within the selected fret range are returned.
Notes:
The higher fret must be larger than the lower fret.
The higher fret must be no larger than 5 frets higher than the lower fret; otherwise, substantially more chords are returned.
Only standard tuning (E, A, D, G, B, e) is supported at this time.
